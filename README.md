# Travel Skill Transfer Planner
Helps travelers identify, plan, and integrate skill development into their journeys — from ceramics in Japan to cooking in Italy.
## What it does
- Performs **skill gap analysis** to match personal growth goals with destination-specific learning opportunities
- Creates **travel-based skill development plans** with pre-trip foundation, in-trip immersion, and post-trip sustainability
- Provides a **transfer strategy framework** to integrate learned skills back into daily life and resume after returning home
## Example scenarios
**1. 出国旅游学了新东西回国怎么继续**
> 👤 我在日本学了茶道，回来发现根本没法复现。还有什么旅途中可以学、回国也能继续的技能？
> 🤖 输出可迁移旅行技能清单——烹饪类（各地菜市场学3个本地菜，回国用替代食材复刻）、手工类（扎染/陶艺带回作品+制作视频留底）、语言类（每日3句实用口语+多邻国巩固）、运动类（留教练联系方式+迪卡侬找同类课程），核心是出发前就想好要学什么、提前预约课程。

**2. Learn ceramics in Japan in 3 weeks**
> 👤 I want to learn ceramics in Japan. Plan a 3-week trip that maximizes skill acquisition.
> 🤖 Skill-acquisition itinerary — pre-trip foundation (wheel-throwing basics online), 2-week intensive workshop placement in Seto/Tokoname, post-workshop practice with local potters, studio-access logistics, and portfolio-documentation plan for continued practice at home.

**3. Design a gap month across Europe**
> 👤 Design a 'gap month' where I learn 3 different skills in 3 different European cities.
> 🤖 Multi-skill itinerary — Barcelona (1 week: Spanish + cooking), Florence (1 week: leather craft), Berlin (1 week: electronic music production) — with vetted workshop providers and skill-transfer-home sustainability plan.
